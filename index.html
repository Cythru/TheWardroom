Our Story

Coastal flavours,
simply done well

Tucked behind Crew Clothing on Fore Street, The Wardroom has been a cherished part of Salcombe's waterfront since the 1980s. Our family became custodians of this beloved café in 2014, embracing its rich heritage while adding our own chapter to its story.

We're more than just a café — we're a place where generations have gathered to share meals, memories, and magnificent estuary views. Whether it's our renowned Crab Benedict, a full Devon breakfast, or artisanal Origin Coffee, every visit is something to savour.

Open Daily from 8:30am

Life on the Estuary

More than breakfast.
More than lunch.

We're a proper waterfront spot — the kind where you linger longer than you planned, order another coffee, and watch the sailboats drift by. Here's what keeps people coming back.

The View

A table with something to say

Perched right on the waterfront with the estuary stretching out ahead of you, every seat feels like the best seat. There's nowhere quite like it in Salcombe — come for the food, stay for the view.

The Food

Devon on a plate, every time

Our crab is hand-picked locally by Excel Crab. Our fish comes from S&J Fisheries. Our meat is from M.C. Kelly down the road. We don't do shortcuts — fresh, local and properly cooked is the only way we know.

The Coffee

Origin Coffee, done right

We've partnered with Origin — one of the UK's most respected specialty roasters — because good coffee matters. Whether it's a flat white at half eight or a post-lunch espresso, we take it seriously.
